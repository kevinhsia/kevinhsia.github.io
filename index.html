Freelance multimedia producer, DJ, & curator of Wine and Vinyl, a monthly music listening party. Based in Chicago, IL. Get in touch.

Photography

Kevin specializes in portrait and event photography. With both agency and freelance experience, he has worked with incredible Chicago artists, brands, and local organizations. Though 35mm film is his forte, he is also a skilled digital photographer and editor.

Videography

Through shoots, edits, and production—Kevin has crafted video concepts for AMC, Marriott, and Serta Mattresses. Working with big and small businesses, Kevin always thinks locally about the organizations that make a difference in the community and how to best tell their story.

Live DJ

Music has always been one of Kevin's greatest passions. In addition to playing some of Chicago’s best spaces, he runs a monthly record listening party called Wine & Vinyl. There’s a different host each month, and is always BYOV: vino and vinyl.
